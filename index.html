About Me

Who I am

Born a gamer, destined to use computers for as long as I walk this earth, I started to follow my dream as a programmer. My freshman year of high school, I remember telling my father “I want to be a game developer”. From that day forth and with a thirst for knowledge, I learned as much about computer programming as I possibly could. Fast forward a couple years into Lone Star College, I had developed a couple games and adored my experience being in charge of a creative team, but as I matured, my interests were refined into the pursuit of becoming a web developer. After spending 6 months focused on fullstack javascript, I knew I made the right choice. My passion lies in computers; it always has, it always will.

What I do

I love problem solving and learning new technologies. My favorite technologies to use in web development are React/Redux, Express, Node.js, and Mongo/Mongoose. I am also constantly working on new projects that I find fun and exciting simultaneously keeping my GitHub updated with the new work.

    "If you love what you do, you'll never work a day in your life." -Dad
